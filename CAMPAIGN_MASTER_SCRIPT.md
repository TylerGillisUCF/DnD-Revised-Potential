# GAME MECHANICS & RULES
## Enhanced D&D Family Adventure System

## CORE PHILOSOPHY: EVERYONE SUCCEEDS!

In this campaign, there is **NO FAILURE**. Every roll succeeds, but HOW you succeed depends on your roll. Dice add excitement, pride, and consequences—not the possibility of game-ending failure.

---

## STAT MODIFIER SYSTEM

### How Stats Work

Your 6 stats (STR, DEX, CON, INT, WIS, CHA) start at 12 points total.
You earn **+1 stat point** after each encounter (ending with 29 points total).

### Stat Modifiers

**Every 3 points in a stat = +1 modifier to relevant rolls**

**Examples:**
- 0-2 points in STR = +0 modifier
- 3-5 points in STR = +1 modifier
- 6-8 points in STR = +2 modifier
- 9-11 points in STR = +3 modifier
- 12+ points in STR = +4 modifier

### When to Add Modifiers

Whenever you roll, add your relevant stat modifier:
- **Combat (STR or DEX):** "Roll d20 + STR modifier"
- **Social (CHA):** "Roll d20 + CHA modifier"
- **Investigation (INT):** "Roll d10 + INT modifier"
- **Wisdom checks (WIS):** "Roll d10 + WIS modifier"
- **Physical challenges (DEX or STR):** "Roll d12 + DEX modifier"

**Your stats GROW throughout the adventure, making you more powerful!**

---

## COIN SYSTEM

### Starting Coins
**0 coins** (you'll earn them throughout your journey!)

### Spending Coins (Strategic Advantages)
- **1 coin:** Reroll any single die
- **2 coins:** Add +3 to any roll (after rolling)
- **3 coins:** Auto-succeed at the highest tier on any non-combat challenge

### Earning Coins
- Complete each Cameo encounter: **+5 coins** (60 coins total from all 12 Cameos)
- Roll a Natural 20: **+1 coin**
- Exceptionally creative solution: DM awards **+1 coin**
- Help another player during their encounter: **+1 coin**
- Win at Gambler encounter: **+5 to +15 coins**
- Perform well at Talent Show: **+5 to +10 coins**
- **TOTAL POTENTIAL: 73-90 coins per player**

### Spending Coins
- Required meals: **20 coins total** (4 meals × 5 coins each, plus 1 free meal)
- Vendor shops: 3-tier pricing (everyone can afford something!)
- Strategic advantages: Rerolls, bonuses, auto-successes
- **You can NEVER go below 0 coins**

### Strategic Choice
Budget wisely! Save for expensive items or spend early for advantages!

## SUCCESS TIERS (ALL ROLLS)

Every roll succeeds, but with different outcomes:

### NATURAL 20 (CRITICAL SUCCESS!)
- Get artifact/reward
- **BONUS:** Gain 1 coin OR advantage on next roll
- Epic narrative outcome
- Everyone celebrates!

### HIGH SUCCESS (15-19 or 13-14 + modifier)
- Get artifact/reward
- Excellent execution
- Feel awesome about it!

### MODERATE SUCCESS (10-14 or 8-9 + modifier)
- Get artifact/reward
- Standard successful outcome
- Clean execution

### LOW SUCCESS (5-9 or 4-6 + modifier)
- Get artifact/reward
- **MINOR SETBACK:** Lose 1 coin OR small disadvantage
- Comedic mishap or learned lesson
- Still succeed!

### CRITICAL FUMBLE (Natural 1)
- Get artifact/reward (you ALWAYS succeed!)
- **HILARIOUS DISASTER:** Lose 1-2 coins
- Succeed in the funniest/most embarrassing way possible
- Everyone laughs!

**Remember: You ALWAYS get the artifact and move forward. Low rolls just add flavor and minor costs!**

---

## COMBAT SYSTEM (VARIED BY ENCOUNTER)

Combat encounters use **DIFFERENT formats** to keep them exciting:

### TYPE 1: CLASSIC DUEL (Best 2 of 3 rounds)
- Each round: Roll d20 + STR (or DEX)
- Enemy rolls d20 + their modifier
- Higher total wins the round
- First to win 2 rounds wins the duel
- Win or lose, you get the artifact!

### TYPE 2: QUICK-DRAW (Single decisive roll)
- One roll only: d20 + DEX
- Beat the target number
- Emphasizes SPEED over endurance

### TYPE 3: ENDURANCE (Survive 3 rounds)
- You don't need to WIN, just SURVIVE
- Roll d20 + CON each round
- Hit target number to withstand attack
- Show resilience, not dominance

### TYPE 4: CHOICE COMBAT (Talk or Fight)
- Choose to fight OR persuade
- If fight: d20 + STR
- If persuade: d20 + CHA
- Both paths succeed!

### Consequences
- **Win combat:** Keep all coins, gain artifact
- **Lose combat:** Get artifact, lose 1 coin (pride cost)
- **Natural 1 in combat:** Lose 2 coins (embarrassing defeat, but still get artifact!)

---

## PUZZLE & INVESTIGATION SYSTEM

### Player Choice + Dice

Puzzles aren't solved by dice alone! YOU make the choice, dice determine execution.

**Example:**
- DM presents 4 colored crystals
- YOU choose the order: "I'll try Blue, Red, Green, Yellow"
- THEN roll d6 to see if your choice works

### Two-Roll Encounters

Some encounters use TWO different dice:
1. **First roll:** Determines your APPROACH
2. **Second roll:** Determines your EXECUTION

### Hints Available
- Spend 1 coin to get a major hint
- Low rolls often give you partial clues
- You can always retry (no failure!)

# DM OPENING NARRATION

**[DM: Before reading the Tavern scene, explain the new mechanics to your players]**

"Welcome to our enhanced D&D adventure! Before we begin, let me explain how this works:

**EVERYONE SUCCEEDS:** There is no failure in this game. Every roll succeeds, but HOW you succeed depends on your dice. Low rolls = funny/minor setbacks. High rolls = epic victories. You ALWAYS move forward.

**STAT MODIFIERS:** Every 3 points in a stat gives +1 modifier. When I say 'Roll d20 + STR', add your STR modifier. You start with small bonuses but grow stronger!

**COINS:** You start with 0 coins. You'll earn coins throughout your journey! Spend them wisely:
- 1 coin = reroll any die
- 2 coins = add +3 to a roll
- 3 coins = auto-succeed (non-combat)

Earn coins by:
- Completing each Cameo encounter (+5 coins)
- Rolling Natural 20s (+1 coin)
- Being exceptionally creative (+1 coin, DM awards)
- Winning at special encounters (Gambler, Talent Show, etc.)

Spend coins at vendors, meals, and for strategic advantages!

**THE QUEST:** You'll each walk a separate path, face 12 encounters, collect 12 artifacts, and grow from 12 to 29 stat points. Then you reunite to save your leader!

Now... let's begin!"

# ACT I - THE TAVERN SCENE (FREE MEAL #1)

## THE JOURNEY TO THE TAVERN

**[DM: Read this to each player individually before they join the group]**

"Welcome, [Player Name]. I'm so glad you made it. Before we begin, I need you to know: You're not just coming to play a game tonight. You're stepping into another world. A world where your choices matter, where your courage will be tested, and where three heroes must walk separate paths before they can reunite to save what they hold most dear.

[Gesture to the hallway trail of candles]

Do you see these lights? They mark the path. Follow them. They will lead you through the Enchanted Forest to the Tavern of Beginnings, where your quest awaits. But remember—you're not entering as [their real name]. Tonight, you are someone else. Someone braver. Someone destined for greatness.

[Lean in slightly, seriously]

When you reach the tavern door, you must knock the special code. Only those who knock correctly may enter. The code is simple:

Knock three times. Pause. Knock twice. Pause. Knock once.

[Demonstrate the rhythm by tapping on your hand: KNOCK-KNOCK-KNOCK... KNOCK-KNOCK... KNOCK]

Three for the three heroes who will walk separate paths. Two for the companions who guide them—Max and Luna. One for the leader you must save.

Can you remember that? Good.

Now go. Follow the candles. Enter the forest. Approach the tavern door. Knock the code. And when I open that door... leave [their real name] behind. You become a hero tonight.

[Gesture dramatically toward the candle trail]

Your quest begins now."
